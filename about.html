Our story

Built inside the plant,
not outside it.

Surendra Engineering Enterprises was founded in 1996 on a simple conviction — that heavy industry runs on partners who show up, perform under pressure and never compromise on safety.

Three decades on, that conviction has scaled into four integrated divisions — Fabrication, Electrical, Civil and Operation & Maintenance — executing live, mission-critical work for aluminium smelters, power plants and infrastructure programs across India.

Today SEE is an ISO 9001, 14001 & 45001 and MSME-certified partnership firm with a turnover trajectory crossing ₹50 Cr, led by Director Surendra Sharma and promoter R.P. Rathore.

Why it compounds

Every contract SEE completes inside a live plant becomes the reference for the next. Procurement teams reward partners who keep production running — and that trust compounds into larger, longer scopes.

The result is a portfolio that has grown nearly 5× in four years, anchored by repeat work for the most demanding industrial buyers in the country.
